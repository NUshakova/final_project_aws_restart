Embroidery Tips

  • Always have fun!
  • Use high-quality thread to ensure durability.
  • Keep your fabric taut in the hoop to avoid puckering.
  • Use sharp needles for clean and precise stitches.
  • Choose the right stabilizer for delicate fabrics.
  • Practice different stitches to enhance your skills.
